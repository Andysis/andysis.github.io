### **Research Interests**  
My research focuses on advancing **surgery robots** and **digestive endoscopy** through cutting-edge artificial intelligence (AI) technologies. By integrating machine learning and computer vision, I aim to enhance surgical precision, improve diagnostic accuracy, and revolutionize healthcare delivery.

#### 1. AI-Driven Surgery Robots
- Developing intelligent algorithms for robotic-assisted minimally invasive surgeries.
- Enhancing robot autonomy through real-time image processing and decision-making systems.
- Improving surgical outcomes by integrating AI with haptic feedback systems.

#### 2. AI in Digestive Endoscopy
- Creating advanced image analysis tools for early detection of gastrointestinal diseases.
- Automating endoscopic procedures to reduce human error and improve efficiency.
- Leveraging deep learning models for real-time lesion segmentation and diagnosis.
